Mid Pacific Naval Theater

Thunder Buddies Studios is building an ambitious maritime theater for Arma Reforger, designed to make open water, warship command, reconnaissance, amphibious support, and large-scale naval conflict matter.

View Showcase Join Discord

The missing battlefield

For years, Arma has delivered infantry combat, armored warfare, and aviation.

True naval warfare has never had a permanent place on the battlefield. The Mid Pacific Naval Theater is designed to change that by turning the ocean from empty space into a living operational front.

Studio objective Our goal is to change that.

Six-month development path

Testing to community release

  1. Month 01 Foundation lock

    Theater scope, art direction, naval objectives, and public showcase language are locked.

  2. Month 02 Sea trials

    Warship movement, ocean staging, reconnaissance flow, and mission pacing are tested internally.

  3. Month 03 Fleet operations

    Open-water engagements, fleet coordination, amphibious support, and command roles are refined.

  4. Month 04 Closed community testing

    Trusted players begin structured playtests and feedback loops through Discord.

  5. Month 05 Public technical showcase

    Progress updates, videos, feature notes, and Workshop preparation go public.

  6. Month 06 Community release

    Release candidate, server readiness, Workshop links, and launch-night operations.
